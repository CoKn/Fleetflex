## Figma Make Prompt

Create a polished clickable web app prototype for a B2B energy platform called **FleetFlex**.

**Concept:**
FleetFlex helps airports use their parked electric vehicle fleet as an energy buffer. The platform first protects vehicle readiness, then uses stored EV energy to reduce airport electricity costs, and only sells remaining surplus energy through a market partner such as Mainova. The product is V2B-first and marketplace-second: airport energy optimization first, external energy trading second.

The prototype should feel like a professional SaaS dashboard for airport energy managers and fleet operators.

---

### Product narrative

FleetFlex solves this problem:

* Airports are electrifying their fleets, but many EV batteries sit idle between operational tasks.
* Electricity prices fluctuate during the day.
* The airport wants to charge vehicles when electricity is cheap, use stored battery energy when airport electricity is expensive, and sell only surplus capacity externally.
* The platform must always protect vehicle readiness and battery health.

The challenge context is a B2B V2G marketplace where fleet operators offer battery capacity and energy buyers purchase flexibility. 
The industry context supports V2B as a practical first implementation for fleet operators with on-site charging infrastructure.

**FleetFlex — Turn parked airport EVs into an energy asset**

**Charge cheap. Power the airport. Sell the surplus.**

# Design style

Use a professional, modern B2B SaaS look.

Visual direction:

* Clean white background
* Dark navy text
* Green and electric blue accents
* Rounded cards
* Subtle shadows
* Clear KPI cards
* Charts and timeline blocks
* Airport + energy + EV iconography
* Avoid consumer-app styling; make it feel enterprise-ready

Tone: trustworthy, operational, financial, precise.

# Key message to communicate throughout the prototype

FleetFlex does not simply sell airport vehicle energy to the grid.

It follows this priority logic:

1. **Protect vehicle readiness**
2. **Reduce airport electricity costs**
3. **Sell only verified surplus energy**

Add this priority logic as a small persistent banner or visual stepper across the dashboard.
